#  E-Commerce Growth Analytics Dashboard

> **Portfolio Project #01**  
> An end-to-end Growth Analytics project built using **Google Analytics 4, BigQuery, SQL, and Looker Studio**.

---

## Overview

This portfolio project simulates a real-world analytics engagement for an e-commerce business.

Using the **Google Merchandise Store (GA4 Public Dataset)**, I designed a centralized reporting solution that transforms raw event data into actionable business insights for executives, marketers, and growth teams.

The project demonstrates how data can be leveraged to improve business performance, marketing effectiveness, and customer conversion.

---

## Business Problem

The business lacked a centralized reporting solution capable of answering key business questions such as:

- How is the business performing?
- Which marketing channels generate the most revenue?
- Which acquisition channels deliver the highest-quality traffic?
- Where are customers dropping off before purchasing?

Without unified reporting, decision-making relied on fragmented data and manual analysis.

---

## Solution

Designed and developed a three-page executive reporting dashboard.

### Executive Overview

Designed for executives and business owners.

**Includes**

- Revenue
- Purchases
- Users
- Conversion Rate
- Average Order Value
- Revenue per Visitor
- Revenue Trend
- Revenue by Traffic Source
- User Distribution by Device

---

### Marketing Performance

Designed for marketing and growth teams.

**Includes**

- Revenue by Channel
- Users by Channel
- Conversion Rate by Channel
- Revenue per Visitor
- Channel Performance Analysis

---

### Funnel & Conversion Analysis

Designed for product and growth optimization teams.

**Includes**

- Customer Purchase Funnel
- Add-to-Cart Rate
- Checkout Rate
- Purchase Rate
- Funnel Drop-Off Analysis

---

## Key Results

| KPI | Value |
|------|------:|
| Revenue | $362,165 |
| Purchases | 5,692 |
| Users | 270,154 |
| Conversion Rate | 2.11% |
| Average Order Value | $63.63 |
| Revenue per Visitor | $1.34 |

---

## Key Insights

- Google generated the highest overall revenue.
- Direct traffic produced a higher Revenue per Visitor than Google.
- Product discovery represented the first major funnel bottleneck.
- Checkout completion presented the largest conversion optimization opportunity.
