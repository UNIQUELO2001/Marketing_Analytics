# Executive Summary

An e-commerce company required a centralized analytics solution to better understand business performance, marketing effectiveness, and customer purchasing behavior.

Using Google Analytics 4 event data stored in BigQuery, I designed a reporting solution consisting of executive dashboards that transform raw data into actionable business insights.

The solution enables stakeholders to monitor key business metrics, evaluate marketing channels, and identify customer journey bottlenecks.

---

# Business Challenges

Prior to this project, stakeholders experienced several reporting challenges:

- Business metrics were spread across multiple reports.
- Marketing performance lacked centralized reporting.
- Customer journey visibility was limited.
- Funnel drop-offs were difficult to identify.
- Executives required faster access to KPIs.

---

The project focused on five primary objectives:

# Business Findings

## Finding 1

Google generated the highest overall revenue and remained the largest acquisition source during the reporting period.

---

## Finding 2

Direct traffic produced a higher Revenue per Visitor than Google, indicating stronger purchase intent among returning customers.

---

## Finding 3

Customer discovery represented the first major funnel bottleneck, suggesting opportunities to improve landing pages, navigation, and product discovery.

---

## Finding 4

Less than half of users who initiated checkout completed a purchase, making checkout optimization the highest-impact opportunity.

---

# Recommendations

## 1. Improve Product Discovery

Increase the percentage of visitors reaching product pages by optimizing:

- Landing Pages
- Navigation
- Internal Search
- Product Categories

---

## 2. Optimize Checkout

Improve checkout completion by reviewing:

- Payment Experience
- Shipping Transparency
- Mobile Checkout
- Form Complexity

---

## 3. Scale High-Value Traffic Sources

Continue investing in acquisition channels that generate strong revenue while expanding high-converting sources with higher Revenue per Visitor.
